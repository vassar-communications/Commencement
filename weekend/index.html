Asian Pacific Islander (API) Graduation Ceremony

Alumnae/i of Asian and Pacific descent (APAVC) and faculty will join seniors to offer congratulations and mark their transition to the world outside Vassar. This annual ceremony welcomes the seniors to the Vassar alumnae/i community. Red satin stoles are mailed to identifying seniors.

Latinx Student Union (LSU) Sarape Ceremony

Alumnae/i of Latinx descent and faculty will join identifying seniors for a celebration marking their transition to the world outside Vassar. 4:00pm

Baccalaureate Service

The Baccalaureate Service, sponsored by the Council of ALANA Seniors and celebrating the 30th anniversary this year, is rooted in the traditions of the African diaspora, and celebrates faith in recognition of academic achievement. It takes a multicultural approach to acknowledge the diversity of the community at Vassar. Open to the entire community of graduates and families.
